# Privacy Policy

## Introduction
This Privacy Policy explains how Bhakti Video Generator handles your data to deliver the service responsibly.

## Data We Collect
We collect account details, preferences, usage events, and content you upload or generate. Diagnostic logs may be retained to improve reliability.

## Third-Party Services (Stripe, S3/MinIO)
We use Stripe to process payments; Stripe may receive billing-related information. We store media assets on S3-compatible storage (e.g., MinIO) for reliability and performance.
